What it is

One operating surface.

Cortex fuses three things that usually live in separate apps — an orchestrator brain, persistent memory, and a voice — into a single desktop environment. You don't open a chatbot. You inhabit an OS where agents do the work.

Orchestrate

A central brain routes tasks to specialist agents and keeps them on mission.

Remember

File-based memory that persists across sessions — context that compounds.

Speak

A living voice interface. Talk to your OS; it talks back and takes action.

The modules

Everything attaches to the spine.

Six parts of one system. Hermes — the messenger — runs the spine that carries every request, reply, and result between them.

hermes://spine

Presence

The Orb

An ambient presence across the whole OS. It listens, it speaks, and it shows you what the agents are doing — while work runs, activity satellites orbit it. One glance at the orb and you know the state of your system.

Voice

Voice loop

Talk to your OS. A wake word summons it; it answers — and then it acts. Ask for something and the loop closes: heard, understood, done, reported back in its own voice.

Companions

The Pantheon

The agents are companions — specialists, each bearing a constellation sigil, ranked in three tiers. You don't manage prompts; you dispatch companions suited to the work.

Olympian

tier i — the principals

Deep-reasoning agents trusted with whole missions. They plan, delegate, and answer for the outcome.

Daimon

tier ii — the intermediaries

Focused specialists that own a domain — research, code, markets — and report upward.

Automaton

tier iii — the hands

Small, fast workers that execute single tasks — fetch, file, format — and return.

Every message between you and the companions passes through Hermes — the messenger runs the spine.

Memory

Neural Map

Memory you can walk through. A full-tab, living knowledge graph grown from file-based memory that persists across sessions — what the agents learn compounds instead of resetting.

Operations

Mission Control

One board for everything in flight — tasks, todos, and the agents running them. What's queued, what's active, what's done: a single view, not five tabs.

Routing

OmniRoute

Any request, the right mind. Cortex routes each task to the model or agent best suited to it — Claude, GLM, Grok, or a local model — without you switching apps or thinking about it.
